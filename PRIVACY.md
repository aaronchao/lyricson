# LyricsOn — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-22_

LyricsOn ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains
what the app does and does not do with information.

## Information the app accesses

- **Currently-playing track metadata.** With your permission (notification
  access), the app reads the active media session to get the **title and artist**
  of the song you are playing. It does not read, store, or transmit the content
  of your notifications.
- **Lyric lookups.** The track title and artist are sent over an encrypted
  (HTTPS) connection to third-party lyric and artwork services — LRCLIB, Genius,
  Netease, QQ Music, and Apple's iTunes Search — solely to retrieve matching
  lyrics and cover art. A web search fallback may be used to find lyrics that
  these services do not have.

## Information the app does NOT collect

- No accounts, names, emails, or passwords.
- No analytics or tracking.
- No advertising.
- No location, contacts, photos, files, or device identifiers.

## Storage

Fetched lyrics are cached locally on your device to make the app faster. This
data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from
anyone.
